📜 Purpose

LienTrace establishes a public, time-stamped registry for claims, testimony, evidence, and asset interpretation across digital systems.

🧠 Human Testimony Layer

Testimony is treated as a structured, time-stamped account of observed experience. It may be reviewed, challenged, or corroborated.

🔍 Proof-of-Meaning Layer

Assets may be ambiguous or misrepresented. This layer enables structured human interpretation of what an asset appears to be, based on observed behavior and evidence.
