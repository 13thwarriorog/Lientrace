LienTrace is a public-facing accountability and evidence registry for structured case tracking, notice, unresolved obligation mapping, and guided human statement intake.

📜 Statement of Purpose

This registry establishes a transparent, publicly accessible record of observed blockchain activity, associated risks, and unresolved accountability concerns. Cases may include direct on-chain observations, corroborated findings, technical inferences, pending disclosures, and public notice entries.

🧠 System Capabilities

  • Public accounting of claims
  • Evidence-linked accountability
  • Disclosure tracking
  • Settlement lifecycle recording
  • Public protection registry
  • Multi-case indexing
  • Status-based evidence review
  • Guided human statement drafting

⚠️ Public Notice

Interaction with referenced assets, contracts, counterparties, or wallets should be conducted with caution pending independent verification and full disclosure.

📊 Evidence Classification

  • Observed → Direct on-chain data or captured system output
  • Corroborated → Supported by multiple independent references
  • Inferred → Technical conclusion based on observed facts
  • Pending Disclosure → Awaiting records, statements, or responses
  • Disputed → Counterparty response or contradiction noted

🧾 Human Statement Entry

LienTrace accepts structured human statements to document suspicious asset behavior, failed swaps, unusual contract restrictions, proxy-only visibility, bot-driven trading, value suppression, tax traps, and unresolved authenticity concerns.

Suggested public statement format:

Statement ID: User-defined or pending

Date (UTC): [enter date]

Wallet Address: [enter wallet]

Network: [BSC / ETH / Polygon / other]

Situation Summary
I received or interacted with the following token(s): token name, contract if known, and source.

Issue Encountered
Describe high tax, blocked sale, unusual trading activity, proxy-only visibility, missing value representation, or other abnormal restrictions.

Observations
Approximate transactions observed, notable bot behavior, associated wallets or contracts, and anything else visible.

Questions / Concerns
Where is the authentic contract? Is the asset freely tradable? Is value being artificially controlled?

Evidence Status
Observed / Corroborated / Inferred / Pending Disclosure

🧾 Filing Workflow

  1. Open case
  2. Attach observable evidence
  3. Classify findings
  4. Issue public notice
  5. Record rebuttal, disclosure, or silence
  6. Mark resolved, unresolved, or archived
